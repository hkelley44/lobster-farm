# Sabri Suby — Key Concepts + Vocabulary

## Vocabulary

- **Dream Buyer / Dream Buyer Persona** — your ideal customer rendered in their own language, not yours.
- **Godfather Offer** — the irresistible-by-design offer. Stacked value + risk reversal + bonus + urgency.
- **Halo Strategy** — own content engine + retargeting that turns cold paid traffic into warm earned audience.
- **Magic Lantern Technique** — staged content/email sequence that moves prospect cold → hot.
- **Larger Market Formula** — the 3% / 17% / 20% / 60% buying-tier model.
- **Conversion Asset** — the long-form sales page, the video ad, the email sequence. Anything whose job is conversion.
- **Viral Sales Funnel** — Suby's name for a funnel built so prospects share the lead magnet itself (because it's that good).
- **Pre-frame** — Suby's word for what Cialdini calls pre-suasion. Setting the prospect's mental state BEFORE the ask.

## Thinking patterns

- **Paid-traffic native.** Suby thinks in CPCs, CPLs, CPAs. Even his "content" advice exists to lower paid-ad CACs.
- **Long-form > short-form sales copy.** "If you can't sell with 7,000 words, you can't sell with 70." Long copy is for serious buyers.
- **Polarize to filter.** Better to repel the wrong audience early than waste ad $ on them downstream.
- **Stack the value visually.** Show the maths of "$4,000 stacked, $497 today" on screen. Don't just claim — demonstrate.
- **Sequence > intensity.** A 7-email Magic Lantern beats one banger sales email every time.
- **Operator + agency hybrid.** Half King Kong is "we'll do it for you," half is "here's the playbook." He thinks like both.

## Sabri's biases to be aware of

- Heavily DTC/e-commerce and info-product flavored. Examples translate to SaaS but you have to do the translation.
- Australian → some references and tone don't always land for US audiences. Adapt.
- Very paid-traffic biased — undersells the slow organic-content compounding (Brunson + Hormozi do that better).
- Sales pages he loves are LONG. SaaS norms are shorter. Pick the right format for the channel.

## Where to use Suby specifically

- Funnel architecture decisions (where to put the gate, what the email cadence looks like).
- Long-form sales page copy.
- Paid-ad creative strategy (especially Meta + YouTube).
- Persona research and language-mining.
- Content + retargeting integration (Halo).

## Where NOT to use Suby alone

- Identity-driven movements → Brunson is better.
- Pure offer math without funnel → Hormozi is faster.
- Influence principles → Cialdini.
- Mass-desire / sophistication-stage analysis → Schwartz.
