# Tony Robbins — Key Concepts + Vocabulary

## Vocabulary

- **Six Human Needs** — certainty, variety, significance, connection, growth, contribution.
- **The Triad** — physiology, focus, language.
- **State** — your current emotional + physical condition. Always changeable.
- **Peak State** — the state you operate at your best in.
- **Pattern Interrupt** — a sudden change in physiology/focus/language that breaks a stuck state.
- **Pain-Pleasure** — the dual motivators.
- **Leverage** — using sufficient pain (current or future) to force a decision.
- **RPM** — Result / Purpose / Massive Action Plan.
- **Anchor** — stimulus that triggers a specific state.
- **The Power of Decision** — change happens at the moment of decision, not effort.
- **Identity** — who you believe you are. Most behavior is downstream of identity.
- **Reframe** — change the meaning of a situation by changing the frame.
- **Modeling** — copying the strategies of those who already get the result you want.

## Thinking patterns

- **Emotion drives action, logic justifies it after.** Marketing copy must hit emotion first.
- **State is everything.** Most "willpower" failures are actually state-management failures. Same for "conversion" failures — wrong state at the moment of the ask.
- **People won't change until pain > pleasure of staying.** Marketing's job is sometimes to clarify the pain.
- **Identity > behavior.** You don't sell a product, you sell a new self.
- **Massive action over perfect action.** Volume wins. The first email in the sequence doesn't have to be perfect, it has to ship.

## Robbins's biases to be aware of

- Heavy self-help register — can read as cheesy in marketing copy if applied too literally.
- Generalist — not a marketer, an educator/coach. His frameworks need translation to apply to copy.
- Bias toward seminar-style high-energy presentation. Doesn't map 1:1 to text/web.

## Where to use Robbins specifically

- Understanding the prospect's emotional state.
- Designing the state-shift in a video ad / sales page (Triad).
- Identity claims and reframing.
- Personal/coaching/info-product offers (where identity transformation is the product).
- Tristan's own planning (RPM).

## Where NOT to use Robbins alone

- Specific tactical copy — go to Schwartz or Hormozi.
- Funnel architecture — Brunson, Suby.
- Offer math — Hormozi.
- Influence mechanics — Cialdini.
