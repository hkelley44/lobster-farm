# Cold Traffic X (Twitter) Ad Templates

X is the right cold paid channel for CombatCall — MMA Twitter is large, engaged, and bettors live there. Less polished than Meta; more direct-response.

## Format options

1. **Promoted Tweet** — looks like an organic tweet.
2. **Promoted Thread** — multi-tweet, deeper content. Higher engagement; harder to scale.
3. **Promoted Video** — best for cold awareness.

## Template structure (promoted tweet, single)

```
[Hook line — pattern-interrupt or stat]
[Body — 1-2 lines]
[CTA — link + benefit]

[Image OR short video]
```
