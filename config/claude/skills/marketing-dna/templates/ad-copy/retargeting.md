# Retargeting Ad Templates

For warm audiences — people who already hit your content, landing page, or app but didn't convert. Different copy logic than cold.

## Audience segments to retarget

1. **Hit landing page, didn't email-capture** — they're Stage 2-3 still. Re-hit with a different value prop.
2. **Email-captured, didn't subscribe** — they're Stage 3-4. They know the product. Hit with offer + social proof + urgency.
3. **Started checkout, abandoned** — they're Stage 5. Hit with offer + guarantee + ONE objection-killer.
4. **Subscribed once, cancelled** — Win-back. Hit with what's changed since they left.

## Frequency caps

- Don't show the same prospect the same ad more than 3 times in 7 days. Diminishing returns. Ad fatigue.
- Rotate 3-5 creative variations per segment.
- Refresh creative every 4-6 weeks even on winners.

## Cross-platform retargeting

- Meta pixel → Meta retargeting (Facebook + Instagram).
- X pixel → X promoted tweets retargeting.
- YouTube custom audience → YouTube TrueView ads (great for re-engagement; people who watched 50%+ of a Cole pick video are highly qualified).
- Email retargeting in Klaviyo / similar based on email engagement is the cheapest channel — exhaust this first.
