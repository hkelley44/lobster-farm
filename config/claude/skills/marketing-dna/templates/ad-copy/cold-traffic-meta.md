# Cold Traffic Meta (Facebook + Instagram) Ad Templates

For Stage 1-2 awareness traffic. Goal: get the click + an email opt-in. NOT to sell.

## Template structure

```
[HOOK — first line, scroll-stopper]
[AGITATE — 1-2 lines of pain]
[BRIDGE — 1 line of "there's a better way"]
[CTA — clear, specific action + benefit]
[Optional: 1-line credibility line]

[Image/video — see creative guide below]

Headline: [Big promise + specificity]
Description: [Reinforces offer + risk reversal]
CTA Button: Sign Up / Learn More
```

## Creative format guidelines

- **Video > image > carousel** for cold traffic on UFC content. Fight clips capture attention.
- **First 3 seconds** must hook. Cole's face + a quick text overlay works.
- **Captions ALWAYS on.** 85% watch with sound off.
- **Vertical (9:16) for Instagram Reels + Facebook Reels.** Horizontal (16:9) only for in-feed.
- **Brand color** (`#f2c94c` gold) consistently used. Becomes an anchor (Robbins).

## Targeting (Meta)

- Interests: UFC, MMA, BetMGM, FanDuel, DraftKings, Action Network, sports betting.
- Behaviors: Engaged shoppers, mobile-active.
- Look-alikes: once you have 100+ subscribers, build LAL from `marketing_emails`. Way better than interest targeting.
- Exclude: existing subscribers (suppression list).

## Budget + bidding (early stage)

- Start: $50-100/day across 3 creative variants per ad set, 2-3 ad sets.
- Bid: lowest cost, optimize for SIGNUPS (the pixel must fire on EmailGate completion).
- Kill creative under <2% CTR or >$5 CPL after 1,000 impressions.
- Scale creative over $3 CPL or >5% CTR by 20%/day.

## Compliance

- Gambling ads on Meta require pre-approval (Gaming and Gambling category). Cole must register CombatCall as a gambling advertiser in Meta Business Manager.
- WA + states where CombatCall is restricted: exclude in targeting.
- 18+/Gamble Aware disclaimer in every ad's primary text.
- Don't make earnings claims. ("Make money" → "Bet smarter.")
