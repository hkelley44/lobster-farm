# TikTok / Reels / Shorts Script Templates

Short-form vertical video. Different rules than X — visual + audio first, text second.

## TikTok / Reels rules for CombatCall

- 15-45 seconds optimal. Over 60 sec = drop-off.
- HOOK in first 2 seconds. If they don't lock in, they scroll.
- Captions on (85% watch muted).
- Vertical 9:16. Always.
- Use trending sounds where they fit. Don't force.
- Post pre-card (Wed/Thu) for max relevance.
- TikTok algorithm rewards consistency: 4-5x/week minimum.

## CombatCall TikTok strategy

The product (a betting analytics tool) is harder to sell on TikTok than direct response on X. TikTok's role for CombatCall = TOP OF FUNNEL awareness + halo content for retargeting on Meta/YouTube.

DON'T try to convert from TikTok directly. DO drive to email opt-in.

## Format guidelines

- **Camera:** Cole's face on screen at least 50% of the time. People follow people.
- **B-roll:** fight clips (used carefully — copyright), data visualizations, ledger screenshots, model dashboard.
- **Text overlay:** big, bold, white on dark. Captions for every word.
- **Sound:** lower the music way down during VO. Bass-y trending sound is fine in background.
- **Outro:** 3-second hold on "combatcall.com" text + CTA.

## Cadence

- 4-5 videos/week sustained.
- Mix: 2 pick reveals (pre-card), 1 receipt (post-card), 1-2 insights (evergreen).
- Reposting to Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts on same schedule. Cross-post natively, not via repost watermarks.

## Goal metrics

- View completion >50%.
- Save rate >2%.
- Profile click-through >1.5%.
- Link in bio CTR >0.5%.

If under these, recut hooks. Hook is 90% of the algorithm signal.

## Gambling content guidelines

- TikTok restricts gambling content. Cole's account needs to be a creator/business account, not personal.
- 18+ targeting + community guidelines compliance.
- Don't guarantee results. Don't show winnings counters unless you have a real, verifiable, public ledger to back them. (Specific dollar P&L numbers are fine ONLY when they're true and citable — Cole hasn't tracked exact P&L, so DO NOT use any specific recovery/profit number until that changes.)
- Add #responsiblebetting / @1800gambler attribution in caption.
