## Tweet-level rules

- First tweet of a thread MUST hook. No "Here's a thread on..." Start with the punch.
- Length: 7-12 tweets is the sweet spot for engagement.
- Add ONE image or chart per thread (raises impressions ~2x).
- End with a soft CTA, not a hard sell. URL in last tweet.
- Reply to EVERY comment for the first 24 hours. Algorithm rewards engagement.
- Quote-tweeting your own thread the next day = free re-amplification.

## Cadence

- Pick thread: every UFC card (~weekly during season).
- Receipt thread: post-card (~weekly).
- Insight thread: 1x per week, off-card days.
- Single-tweet hot takes during fights: live tweeting fight nights builds engagement massively. Cole live-tweets every card.
